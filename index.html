The RHB Way Service Behaviours

I own it and get it right

  • Deliver on our promise
  • Keep customers informed
  • When obstacles arise, escalate issues promptly
  • Ensure every question is answered

01

  • Make the process effortless
  • See the customer journey through our customer's eyes
  • Turn barriers into solutions

02

  • Explain the “why” behind every decision
  • Use plain language and be transparent

  • Turn failures into lessons

The ground rules

Simple by design

Who can nominate

Any people manager, own direct reports only. One per month. No self or skip-level.

Who decides

Leaders pick recipients at their discretion. Story-led — no scoring, no repeat achievers.

Who can shout out

Everyone. The Viva Engage community is open to all staff. Praise generously.

Who runs it

Group Customer Experience — the tracker, the LED showcase, the email, the AI video.
