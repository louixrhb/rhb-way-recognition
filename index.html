Story Coach

Let Copilot interview you

A strong nomination is a strong story. Copy this prompt into Microsoft 365 Copilot and it will interview you, one question at a time, then draft the story for your form.

Open M365 Copilot ↗
Copy-paste prompt
You are my story coach for an RHB Way Recognition nomination. Interview me one question at a time about: (1) what my team member did, (2) the impact on the customer or team, and (3) which RHB Way Service Behaviour it shows — I Own It & Get It Right · I Make It Easy For Others · I Listen To Understand & Communicate Clearly · I Stay Curious & Adapt Fast. Then write a vivid 120–180 word nomination story in my voice.

The four behaviours

Tie the story to at least one

I Own It & Get It Right

Accountability, start to finish.
  • Take personal responsibility from first contact to resolution
  • Do it right the first time, every time
  • Follow through until the matter is fully closed
  • Own mistakes fast — and fix them faster

I Make It Easy For Others

Remove the friction.
  • Simplify processes, forms and answers
  • Anticipate what's needed next
  • Remove obstacles instead of passing them on
  • Make the next person's job easier — colleague or customer

I Listen To Understand & Communicate Clearly

Hear first. Then be heard.
  • Listen fully before responding
  • Ask questions to uncover the real need
  • Use simple, jargon-free language
  • Confirm understanding before closing the conversation

I Stay Curious & Adapt Fast

Learn it. Apply it. Today.
  • Keep learning new tools, products and skills
  • Welcome feedback and act on it
  • Experiment, improve, and share what works
  • Meet change with speed and a positive mindset

Print the letter and present it face to face every Wednesday. The moment matters more than the paper.

Capture the moment

The ground rules

Simple by design

Who can nominate

Any people manager, for their own direct reports only. One nomination per manager per month. No self-nominations, no skip-level nominations.

Who decides

SBG/SFG leaders select commendation recipients entirely at their discretion. Story-led — no scoring matrix, no quota, and no repeat achievers.

Who can shout out

Everyone. The Viva Engage community is open to all staff, with no forms and no tie to the formal stream. Praise generously.

Who runs it

Group Customer Experience (GCX) owns the campaign — the tracker, the LED showcase, the monthly email, and the year-end AI video.
